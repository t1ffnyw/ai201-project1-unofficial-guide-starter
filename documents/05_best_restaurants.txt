Since you're visiting just try our local food. No real reason for you to try our best steak house or fine dining establishments as you can get that stuff elsewhere.

Go down to LP Steamers in locust point and order their sampler. They aren't the absolute best crabs around (they're good though), but the sampler lets you get a taste of a lot of our local seafood options.

Go to Faidley's and get a jumbo lump crab cake, those are a big thing here in Baltimore. Tons of places offer crab cakes of varying quality. I'd say Faidley's has a decent argument for being the best in the city.

If you can get outside the city go to Chap's Pit Beef and try out a pit beef sandwich. That's Baltimore's take on BBQ. Don't forget to put the sauce of your choice on it.
