[wbruce098, -9 pts] Nah, gotta make that shit yourself! Find a few hours, some good ingredients: grab a can of San Marzano tomatoes (diced, preferably), tomato paste, fresh basil, lots of minced garlic, salt, pepper, and you’ve got an amazing sauce. Don’t skimp on the basil and garlic. Let it all simmer ~20 mins or so. For spaghetti, brown some 80/20 meat (not the super lean stuff unless you’re recovering from or preventing heart issues), salt, pepper, toss in sauce. 

Boil some noodles, drain, and pour a little olive oil into the pot, add garlic (powder is fine here unless you wanna be hella fancy), smoked paprika, maybe butter, mix noods back in. Serve it all together for an orgasmic meal!
